metagraphed indexes every Bittensor subnet — its public APIs, schemas, docs, endpoints,
providers, freshness, and registry gaps — and ships a first-party developer block explorer
over chain-direct data. These docs cover both: the registry's public API and the explorer
surfaces, plus the access layers (RPC, GraphQL, feeds, webhooks, search/AI) built on top.

Everything here is public and needs no API key.

<Callout type="info">
  Looking for the full per-endpoint reference with a live try-it client? See the [API
  reference](/docs/api-reference) — generated straight from the published [OpenAPI spec](/schemas).
</Callout>

## Registry & subnets

- [Subnets](/docs/subnets) — `GET /api/v1/subnets` and its structure, lifecycle, market, and detail resources.
- [Subnet metagraph & validators](/docs/metagraph) — `/subnets/{netuid}/metagraph`, `/validators`, `/neurons`, and history.
- [Subnet catalog](/docs/catalog) — the 129 curated subnets, generated from the registry overlays.
- [Economics](/docs/economics) — `/api/v1/economics` and `/economics/trends`, the dynamic-TAO market data.
- [Health & readiness](/docs/health) — `/api/v1/health`, `/health/history/{date}`, `/health/trends`, and probe-derived uptime.

## Block explorer

- [Blocks](/docs/blocks) — `GET /api/v1/blocks`, block detail/extrinsics/events, and block-production stats.
- [Extrinsics](/docs/extrinsics) — `/api/v1/extrinsics`, `/extrinsics/{hash}`, and governance/config surfaces.
- [Accounts](/docs/accounts) — `GET /api/v1/accounts/{ss58}` and its sub-resources — identity, stake, and activity.
- [Chain events reference](/docs/chain-events) — `GET /api/v1/chain-events`, `/chain-events/stats`, and the block-level event feed.
- [Chain analytics](/docs/chain-analytics) — `/api/v1/chain/*` chain-wide rollups and leaderboards.

## Access & integration

- [RPC](/docs/rpc) — one read-only JSON-RPC URL for Bittensor, health-aware load balancing.
- [GraphQL](/docs/graphql) — shape one request across the registry.
- [Search & AI](/docs/search-ai) — `GET /api/v1/search`, `/search/semantic`, and `POST /api/v1/ask`.
- [Feeds](/docs/feeds) — subscribe to registry changes, incidents, and coverage gaps.
- [Webhooks](/docs/webhooks) — `POST`/`GET`/`DELETE /api/v1/webhooks/subscriptions` for change delivery.
